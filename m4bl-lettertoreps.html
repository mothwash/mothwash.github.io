Dear [Representative],



My name is [name] and I am a constituent from the [District Name] District. What are you doing to protect black lives and BIPOC futures? How are you going to begin to dismantle the racism and white supremacy embedded in our institutions and police culture? We have much work to do as a state to combat systemic racism, and it is beyond time to make a strong start.



I am urging you to consider the following actions:

  • Listen to the experiences coming from communities of color and the policies from which they have been directly penalized (e.g. The New Jim Crow)

  • Budget with different priorities - primarily divesting police militarization funds to interdisciplinary response teams and structural institutions in marginalized neighborhoods and counties that were victims to these racist structures

  • Fight for the removal of qualified immunity that makes police a protected class of citizen

  • Refuse to accept financial contributions from policing unions, i.e. sign No Cash From Cops

  • Push for oversight as elucidated by Campaign Zero

  • Get procedural, independent investigations for prior and present cases of excessive force committed by the police, all of which logged in a secure, national database

  • H.R. 7120, The George Floyd Justice in Policing Act

  • S. 3985, The JUSTICE Act
