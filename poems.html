stapled haiku

office of eggshells

across from byzantine halls

messianic death

a confusing day that i deleted later

it’s your birthday and it sucks

seasonally outside. there stands the fifth

to last

letter for vindictive opportunities,

but your Atlantean impunity,

or is it distance,

leaves a page silent, emptied by

the edict of muddied detachment,

the canorous decree of distance.

please drag me through the blood again

so i can remember

why we wanted to die so much.

mulchy vengeance feeds

the black viper’s bugloss,

take my oxen tongue to bare and

wasted places. wheeled by the chariot

of fuck you, of fuck your freedom,

fuck the tropic of cancer, fuck

i think i love you, fuck my

projections and expectations i could never say,

fuck the downpour we immolated

ourselves in,

just to catch a glimpse of the inextinguishable,

fuck your father, fuck his guts,

fuck satre’s nausea and fuck your liter before noon,

fuck cigarettes after sex and sex

after cocaine socialist pulp, you’re probably

getting fucked in your sleep

(i’m so sorry) and fuck

that emperor in the fast food terrarium,

and fuck your brother still working

for defense without coming to yours,

your fucking pet names i almost

forgot, fuck the milk

of lactating priests and large titted beasts,

fuck those bullshit old friends of mine

and their perforations, perseverations

of your fucked up drowning face that

i can do nothing about, and fuck your

door that i drunkenly slammed so hard

it shattered, and fuck those last few

cigs on my porch when we

looked at each other

and knew we were killing each other

in the honeymoon of merciful martyrdom,



but fuck

i hope you’re content with not

killing yourself.
