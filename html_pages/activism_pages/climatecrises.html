Letter to Reps

Dear [Representative],



My name is [name] and I am a constituent from the [District Name] District. What are you doing to protect the climate and our children's future? How are you going to begin to divest from the corrosive energy sources, challenge the corruption embedded in our institutions, and begin to make lasting sustainable change? We have much work to do as a state to combat the climate crises, and it is beyond time to make a strong start.

I am urging you to consider the following actions:

  • Listen to the experiences coming from communities of color/indigenous communities and the practices that have directly impacted them (e.g. The Atlantic Pipeline, The Nature Gap)

  • Budget with different priorities - primarily divesting polluting energy sources to low carbon emitting and sustainable opportunities

  • Refuse to accept financial contributions from oil, natural gas, and coal

  • Push for oversight as elucidated by UN Sustainable Devlopment

  • Get procedural, independent investigations for prior and present cases of excessive pollution committed by corporations, all of which logged in a secure, national database
