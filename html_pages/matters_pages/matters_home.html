does it really matter?

should i really care?

in playing the game, and showing my willingness to play, i have been let in on some secrets.



resources are abundant

and skittish.

think of the holders of them as county fair judges assessing pigs. they can evaluate the size, but they can't know if this is the pig that breaches evolutionary barriers for the speices



knowledge is infinite

and is constantly being pulled out of thin air

a pig can make an infinite number of thoughts and observations and snort them into the mud and if you'd like to dig for them, you are welcome to do so...



not all patterns matter

and some of them lie.

the intelligence of pigs has been disguised as greed. the violence of wolves have been mistaken for ambition.





what i want is for what matters to me, to matter to those that can do something about it.

pretty fucking boring, but fucking simple

i've spent a lot of time with logic and reason. spent a lot of time accumulating different marginalized voices and perspectives.

we didn't know we could accept queerness. we didn't know smoking would kill us. but we learned.



click here if you haven't learned



click here if you know what i'm talking about









stopping anyone from learning is one of two things

1. the fear, the anxiety, and the thoughts from consequence long ago

2. it doesn't matter to me



if you fall under the former, your anxiety is buying you a one way ticket to a mind cave everytime you have a negative thought associated with what is giving you anxiety.

don't go to the cave. map out what sets you off and make decisions to act differently. it takes practice. commit to the practice.

if you fall into the latter

you're on my fucking site! make it matter.

life does not end at self actualization, it actually is meant to culminate in community actualization.

the indigenous could have taught us that if we didn't commit genocide and distort their ideas to fit the enslaving concept of individuality.

if you're actually curious, ask questions. learn what logic is. define your morality. seek out different perspectives. find the pattern that is human and caring.

hi thanks for coming

there's too much power consolidated right now, out of reach of the common person. but there is hope.

historically, we need to look at unions and revolutions and social movements. we need to seriously evaulate changes in beliefs and the entire histories of those changes.

if you've done the work, you have a great moral compass and a strong understanding of people. we need to take the next step.

i'm searching myself. these old systems and means of resistance won't work in the age of information.

not just history, but stories, the physics and life of these stories, the math and emotion of these stories, the context and spacetime of these stories

go forth. piece the puzzle together. we're running out of time. learn and create and share and talk and learn and create and share and talk...

eventually, we will need to wield both resources and powers of persuasion to take us to the next level. we will weild influence and our knowledge of how people work

finally, if someone is succeeding - build them up

and share their story. keep them balanced and let them lead us forward. we can't be fucking around with humanitarian crises anymore, it's embarassing.

i believe in you. i want us all to have a worthy life. to live in the joy of stories from each other.

the story of you is important too. and i want you to tell it in full,

so fight for freedom and future.
