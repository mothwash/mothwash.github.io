  • chewing hangnails
      prose emotional struggle manic bipolar disorder trauma dark freaky moral lesson water breathing breathe plants frustration sick

      out of a nail bed comes a little bulbous arrow. black and fungal forward. it metastisizes, tumorously tickling at first. the arrowhead separates as if unseaming along its dagger shape and coughs. in the foggy discharge, the ultimate failure and figurative threat appears in the thought of “if this then that.” when pointing anywhere, the arrow wiggles and worms into a spatial direction that seems menaingless, while it is in fact aiming itself with the tide of time. tugging at it is immediately painful. pocketing it is uncomfortable. the foggy coughs accumulate into a foreboding cloud. the noxious miasma continues to paint threateningly, and disguise the culturing length.



      here is what to do. bury the hand in soil, making sure the moisture goes all the way down. blow on the ground as if cooling hot soup. it must be raining in the depictions of failure for this to work, since the arrow must be watered. it will grow longer and creep up. keep your nostrils safe. the knees beneath start to sink a tad and root. fate and shame and emptiness taste a lot like bile. vomit them and feed the cursor. chunks of trauma will come out and the brume will mirror this. this is a hard fruit to bear, but please keep going.



      no standing or wiping while the stalks rise. a leaf will try to choke so chew aggressively. every bite is a future convoluted. every fiber gets soaked with saliva that reclaims nerves. the bile is acid burning the soil and the arrow fights back with a base, pumping alkaline lies and misdirections. stay focused. if the amygdala arrow needs an adrenergic response, it will get the fight. a fight forcing growth and love and the moment down into its slender core. kiss with leaf blood and let it know who’s boss.



      eyes closed now, this does not need to be seen. feel a storm passing. feel a sun leaking. feel a ripe peach dancing on the temple. inhale. slowly lift the chin, stop stretching before you get to neck pain. this is the last stop, so hold it and let breath go. jaws unclench and a bite is taken tasteless, but textured like a rindless citrus fruit with a juice that cools the raw exposure in the throat. feel a friend nearby with shears free the hangnail. pull the hand loose and feel no dirt.
